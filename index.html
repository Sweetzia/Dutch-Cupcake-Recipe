How to make Dutch cupcakes

Two words: Dutch cupcakes. These light, buttery, moist and yummy cupcakes stars three layers of Dutch flag colors that bake up into a beautiful treat for Kingsday and any other celebration.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • food coloring (red,white and blue)
  • cupcake frosting
  • orange sprinkles

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit
  2. Line cupcake tins with wrapper
  3. In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking bowder and salt
  4. Add the butter and sugar in the bowl and mix together until light and fluffy.
  5. Add the eggs to the mix. And after mixing this together add the vanilla.
  6. Divide the batter into three bowls and dye each bowl with a different food coloring
  7. Spoon a portion of the red batter into each cupcake cup, top it with the white and the blue.
  8. Bake the cupakes 15-18 minutes.
  9. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and cool them off
  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, spread the frosting on them, then top the orange sprinkles and serve your Dutch cupcakes.
